Useful links

  • Register with USA Rugby each competitve cycle as a Referee. Make sure you use "Iowa Referees" as your entity! You can dual register as a player with your normal club, but please do referee registration with Iowa Referees. You may also have modules to complete to be Compliant when you register.

  • Our senior and collegiate referee work is managed on RefUp. Register with RefUp to work collegiate and senior side matches. Registrations will need to be approved. For assistance with RefUp, see their Help docs first, then feel free to ask.

  • Report any yellow or red cards for Midwest sanctioned senior competition here. This will be seen by the Iowa and Midwest disciplinary/compliance officers. Additionally, any yellow or red cards in a senior or college side match that are reported in RefUp will be automatically sent to the Iowa Rugby Union Disciplinarian.

  • For High School only use this form to submit scores and settle payments.

  • World Rugby has an app for the Laws of Rugby. Get it so you always have a copy. Download for Apple or for Google.

  • Current World Rugby Law Application Guidelines can be found on their site.

  • USA Rugby Referee Resources has some fantastic resources like Game Management Guidelines, links to important World Rugby clarifications, local USA Rugby policies, and game day type information. It gets updated with some frequency.

  • USA Rugby's Learning Center has a number of webinars saved ranging on topics of diet, stretching, speed work, refereeing youth, foul play, and a number of other topics specific to refereeing.
