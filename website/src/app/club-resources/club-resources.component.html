Policies and information for clubs

  • For any questions on if cleats, mouth guards, braces, tights, etc., are legal, start with the Protective Equipment & Clothing Guidelines from USA Rugby. Any other questions, reach out!
  • Email is the preferred method of contact for your referee. You can find contact info in our Directory.
  • League or non-tournament matches:
    • The Home team is responsible for collecting the following information and sending it to the assigned referee by the Wednesday evening before the match. Failure to do so could result in loss of referee assignemnt.
      • Home jersery, short, and sock color.
      • Visitor jersery, short, and sock color.
      • Directions to the field.
      • Kick off time.
      • Directions to the social (if applicable).
    • Clubs not current on referee fees with the Society may not be able to secure a referee.
    • Clubs without a current and active referee memeber in the Society may be liable to monetary penalty, as per Iowa Rugby Union bylaws, or lower priority for assignments.
