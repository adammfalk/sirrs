COVID-19 Safety Measures

Here are the steps we're taking to help maintain as high of a safety level as possible. These policies apply to Referees, Assistant Referees, and Touch Judges in high school tournaments, and are current as of March 1, 2021.

Some of these measure are not ideal, but nothing about the pandemic is.

Before the match

  • Before leaving for the match, please fill out our Symptom Checker. open_in_new
  • Bring your own water and refrain from sharing water bottles.
  • When arriving at the grounds, please set up your spot in a socially distanced manner.
  • Please wear a mask anytime you are not actively officiating while at the grounds.
  • Pre-match chats (coin toss, front rows, etc.) should be done distanced and while wearing a mask.

During the match

  • Mask wearing is not required while refereeing on field. By all means, feel free to wear one if you feel the need.
  • Assistant Referees or Touch Judges will wear masks due to the proximity of subs/coaches/medics on the sidelines, who are to be masked per the Midwest and USA Rugby. We will use that as our guidance too. Our goal is to limit possible spread as much as we can.
  • Try to keep as much distance as feasible while still maintaining a solid presence, particularly at scrum time. Use your best judgement on balancing rugby saftey with pandemic safety.
  • If possible, use a mask or a gaiter while having close chats during a match, like speaking to a captain about an issue.
  • Refrain from using any team water bottles if offered.

After the match

  • Again, wear a mask when on the sidelines or not actively officiating while at the grounds.
  • It's recommended to wipe down flags with a bleach wipe (or something similar) if sharing that equipment.
