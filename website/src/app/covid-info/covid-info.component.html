COVID-19 Safety Measures

Here are what we're doing to help maintain as high of a safety level as possible. These policies apply to Referees, Assistant Referees, and Touch Judges, and are current as of March 1, 2021.

Before the match Before leaving for the match, please fill out our Symptom Checker. open_in_new Bring your own water and refrain from sharing water bottles. When arriving at the grounds, please set up your spot in a socially distanced manner. Please wear a mask anytime you are not actively officiating. Pre-match chats (coin toss, front rows, etc.) should be done distanced and while wearing a mask. During the match Mask wearing is not required while officiating on field. However, you may choose to do so. Try to keep as much distance as feasible while still maintaining a solid presence. Refrain from using any team water bottles if offered. After the match Wear a mask when on the sidelines or not actively officiating. It's recommended to wipe down flags with a bleach wipe (or something similar) if sharing that equipment.
